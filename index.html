Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Red vs Blue Challenge?
It is an interactive "Would You Rather" game where players choose between two difficult dilemmas. After choosing, you see how your decision compares to the rest of the world.

Are these the best Would You Rather questions?
Yes! We update our database weekly with the most challenging, funny, and deep questions to ensure you never get bored.

Is this game free to play?
Absolutely. You can play the Red vs Blue Challenge for free on any mobile or desktop browser.

How are the percentages calculated?
The percentages represent real-time data from thousands of players globally who have taken the challenge.
